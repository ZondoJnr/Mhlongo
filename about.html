Who is Ndumiso?

Ndumiso Mbonisi Mhlongo is a man of purpose, passion, and divine assignment. With a heart for service and a mind for leadership, Ndumiso wears many hats with grace. He is a professional nurse, seasoned manager in the healthcare system, an engaging author, a captivating public speaker, and a faith-filled coach and preacher.

As a strategic thinker and transformational leader, Ndumiso believes that true change begins with mindset and leadership. His message is simple yet profound: **Lead with intention, serve with compassion, grow with purpose**. Over the years, he has mentored young professionals, guided teams through turbulent transitions, and spoken into the lives of individuals and congregations alike.
